FLEA – bit(e)s and pieces

FLEA is a upcoming first-order theorem prover with equality. For an unsatisfiable set of (non-ground) clauses it will eventually find a unsatisfiable set of ground instances of those clauses. Satisfiability checking modulo equality logic with uninterpreted functions is utilized to confirm (un-)satisfiability of ground clauses and to guide the search for relevant instances of clauses.
