\begin{definition}
	We say a term \( t \) is \coloremph{free} for variable \( x \)
	in a formula \( F \) if for every variable \( y \in \var(t) \)
	and every subformula \( G = \quantify y H \) of \( F \),
	the variable \( x \not\in\fvar(H) \).
\end{definition}
